Overnight Oats

Mason jars: not just for your grandma's jams

Do not show images

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Serving Size: 1

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup dry rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 1/2 cup milk or soy beverage
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Mason jar

Directions

Pour all ingredients into mason jar, starting with the oats. No need to stir.

Close lid and place in fridge before bedtime.

Grab and go in the morning, or if you have more time, pour into a bowl, warm in the microwave for 30 seconds and add fresh fruit like bananas for an extra delicious treat.

Not only is this a fast, simple and healthy way to eat the most important meal of the day, it's very simple to make alterations to suit your taste. Add a tablespoon of chocolate chips to make a pseudo-Reeses mix. Add a tablespoon of chia seeds for that extra boost of energy and fiber, or berries, peaches, granola, yogurt - the options are endless!
